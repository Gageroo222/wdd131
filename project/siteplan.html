Site Name: WDD 131 Trivia Master

Review your html and css skills and compete against others to claim a spot on the leaderboard.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer users an engaging platform to test their knowledge through quizzes, learn new information in the study section, and compete for high scores on the leaderboard.

Scenarios

  • Student: "Is there a study section where I can learn about various web development topics?"
  • Competitive User: "How can I prove my knowledge and get on the top 10 leaderboard?"

Color Scheme

Blue - Used for the header and footer background.

Orange Red - Used for secondary colors.
